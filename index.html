Lazerous SteelFlame
A young and up coming Blacksmith seeking to make the best possible weapons and armour for those around them. Seeks to work under some of the great blacksmiths of the time to learn and master the secrets of the trade. Despite being only 16 he is a hard worker and has plenty to say in terms of smithing. Not much else to say then he is a short 4ft'9 human who is often underestimated and teased because of their size.
